WAQF Program

WAQF – OUR ACHIEVEMENTS AND PROGRAM FOR 2018-19

2015-16
  • Conducted seminar for 125 elite members of the Community.

2016 -17

  • Awareness program through lecture in Masjid & small gatherings.
  • Our directors have attended National seminar on Waqf – 2016 at Bangalore

2017 -18
  • Conducted awareness program on Role & responsibilities of Mutwalli in protecting the Waqf Properties
  • Trained 100 volunteers of which 50 were Imams of the Masjid on the fundamentals of Waqf
  • Educating the community on the Waqf Survey planed by Government of Maharashtra - 2017
  • Coordinating with Collector & Settlement Commissioner in reference with Waqf Survey – 2017
  • Assisting the Mutwallies to complete the formalities in reference to identifying, demarking the Waqf properties
  • Created Advisory committee consisting of 2 retired District Judges, Technical expert to guide the people of Maharashtra on Waqf issues
  • Assist in filing complains with Waqf Board on encroachment & also discussed the issue of Permanent appointment of CEO with the Principal secretary – Minority Affairs - GoM

2018-19
  • To conduct Training Program on Waqf in Different Districts of Maharashtra
  • Pursue with the government of Maharashtra legally and through communication for smooth functioning of Waqf Board
  • Conduct a State Seminar to educate and train in developing Waqf Properties
  • To identify and develop at least one Waqf property by setting up school, hospitals, sports academy etc.
