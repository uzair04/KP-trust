CIVIL SERVICES – OUR ACHIEVEMENTS AND PROGRAM FOR 2018-19 IAS IFS IPS IRS

Objective: To groom the members of the community for different competitive exams, preferably UPSC.
2014-16
  • Sponsorship program for the year 2019-20.
  • Exam for sponsorship shall be conducted on 10th February 2019 (Sunday) in list of cities identified
  • Graduate Students Or Final year of Graduation, below the age of 30 shall be eligible for register for exam at www.kptrust.in
2016 -17
  • Our directors visited 16 cities of Maharashtra to familiarize the members of the community on Career in Civil Services
  • Conducted Civil Service awareness program at Nehru Memorial hall Pune , inviting IPS , IRS officers and Dy. Director Corporate Law to address the program.
  • Started own coaching institute in Pune, provided coaching for 13 students.
2017 -18
  • Our directors visited 23 cities of Maharashtra to familiarize the members of the community on Career in Civil Services.
  • Induction program for new batch and Felicitation of Ms Muskan Pathan – ( Topper of ICSE – 2017) by Lt. General P. M. Hariz (General Officer Commanding – in – Chief Southern Command)
  • Conduct Civil Service awareness program at Nehru Hall – Ghule road, program addressed by Sr. IRS and two new Civil Service officers.
  • Sponsored partly / fully 16 students for one year Civil Services Residential Coaching program, costing Rs 1.5 Lack per student .
2018-19
  • Our directors visited 16 cities of Maharashtra to familiarize the members of the community on Career in Civil Services.
  • Likely to provide one year residential coaching to 15 to 20 students aspiring to make their career in Civil Services
Achievements

  • Mr Danish Baig, sponsored for coaching in Delhi, Passed Prelims – 2017, did not clear mains exam.

Process for Registration for Exam

  • Sponsorship program for the year 2019-20.
  • Exam for sponsorship shall be conducted on 10th February 2019 (Sunday) in list of cities identified
  • Graduate Students Or Final year of Graduation, below the age of 30 shall be eligible for register for exam at www.kptrust.in
  • Syllabus for the exam shall be Civil Service Prelims pattern ( Objectives, Essay, Group Discussion / Personal Interview)
  • Coaching shall commence on 1st of July 2019 and shall get over end by April 2020.
  • Candidates selected cannot involve into any exam / coaching / education etc during the training period.
  • Candidate can be eligible for second year of coaching if found capable.
  • Sponsorship shall be at the desecration of the Trust.
Sponsorship cost includes cost of (Coaching + Accommodation) Candidate shall pay for the cost of food only
